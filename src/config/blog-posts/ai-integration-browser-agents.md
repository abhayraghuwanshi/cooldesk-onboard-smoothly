# AI Integration: Transforming Your Browser into an Agentic Operating System

The browser is no longer just a window to the internet—it's becoming a platform for autonomous agents. This shift from "browsing" to "executing" is the biggest change in computing since the smartphone.

## From Extensions to Agents

For a long time, AI integration meant having a chatbot in a sidebar. While helpful, this is passive. The next level—"Agentic AI"—is active. It can understand your environment, make decisions, and take actions on your behalf.

### The Elements of a Modern AI Integration

To be truly useful, AI needs more than just a large model. It needs:
-   **Vision and Awareness**: The ability to "see" the DOM and understand what a button does.
-   **Memory**: A record of your preferences and past interactions (safely stored locally).
-   **Tools**: The ability to interact with web APIs, scrapers, and local storage.
